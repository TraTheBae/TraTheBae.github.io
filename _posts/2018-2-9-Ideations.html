Setting: The Setting for my game will take place on a small mountain full of Monks. The Goal: The Goal of the game will be to be nice to the monks and learn about the monks and their culture The things to collect: Inner peace. A key to the secret monk portal. The Holy water of the Monk Gods. How you Loose:You anger the monks and they kill you and slowly burn you over a open fire. You get to an end screen where it says you loose because of a certain reason. How you win: You safely travel home safely and make friends with the monks and they give you a sip of the holy god water.
